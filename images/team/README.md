# Team Photos

Add your team photos here. The website will automatically display them in the Team Gallery section.

## Recommended:
- Team Diwali celebration photos
- Team events and gatherings
- Collaboration moments
- Team achievements

## File Format:
- JPEG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600px or larger
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 works best

## How to Use:
1. Add your photos to this folder
2. Update the HTML in `index.html` to reference your images:
   ```html
   <div class="gallery-item">
       <img src="images/team/your-photo.jpg" alt="Team Photo">
   </div>
   ```
